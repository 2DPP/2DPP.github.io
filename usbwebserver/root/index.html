What is ProblemPal?

ProblemPal is a desktop application that aims to give a spotlight to the problems that are not addressed in STI College Las Piñas.

Our Mission

We in the ProblemPal team created this app in order to highlight hidden problems in our school, and by highlighting these problems we hope that the members of our community help towards resolving these issues to make the school better for everybody.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a place where individuals at STI College Las Piñas can voice their complaints anonymously. We want to make the community help each other solve problems that go unnoticed, making the school a more positive environment.

Features

Complaint feed

Users are able to post complains using the post bar. The posts that are uploaded will show up in this "complaint feed".

Admin Approval

In order to prevent spamming or posts that are unrelated to problems in school, there will be assigned administrators that will monitor and approve whatever post is submitted. All posts will go first into this approval section before it shows up in the complaint feed. Submitted posts will only show up in the complaint feed if an admin approves of it.
